Hide & Seek with Cap'n Vor

The Lotus has awoken you early, Tenno. Captain Vor is coming to kill you, you must evade him as long as possible!

Your goal is to hide from Captain Vor while he searches for you. Since you've just awoken, most you have little in the way of health and energy, a direct confrontation is not reccomended.

Use WASD to move, space to jump and shift to crouch. Click and drag to rotate the camera.

ESC to pause.
